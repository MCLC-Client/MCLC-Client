Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 21, 2026

1 Information Collection

MCLC Launcher is designed with privacy in mind. We collect minimal information necessary to provide our services. This includes technical data like your operating system version and launcher logs to help us troubleshoot issues.

2 Account Data

When you sign in with your Microsoft account, we use OAuth2 for authentication. We do not store your password. Your access tokens are stored locally on your machine and are only used to authenticate session requests with Mojang and Microsoft servers.

3 Analytics

We may use anonymized analytics to understand how many users are active and which features are most used. This data is never linked to your personal identity and is used solely to improve the MCLC experience.

4 Data Security

We implement a variety of security measures to maintain the safety of your personal information. Your local data (instances, logs, settings) remains on your device unless you explicitly use cloud backup features (if available).

Contact Us

If you have any questions regarding this privacy policy, you can contact us via our Discord Support Server.
